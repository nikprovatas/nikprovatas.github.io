About Me

I am a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Janea Systems, building production LLM-powered systems — from fine-tuned transformer models and multi-agent RAG pipelines to real-time inference serving on Kubernetes. I specialize in end-to-end ML: LoRA/QLoRA fine-tuning, model serving via vLLM/KServe, evaluation frameworks, and observability stacks that keep systems honest in production.

Before focusing on LLMs, I spent several years designing large-scale data and streaming platforms — architecting Spark Structured Streaming pipelines on Databricks processing millions of events daily, building Kafka-based real-time ingestion systems, and optimizing Delta Lake storage at terabyte scale. This data engineering foundation — ETL/ELT orchestration, distributed systems reliability, and petabyte-scale pipeline management — informs how I build and operate ML infrastructure today.

I hold a PhD in Distributed Deep Learning from NTUA (2025), with 10 peer-reviewed publications, and am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Quantum Computing and Quantum Technologies at Democritus University of Thrace.
